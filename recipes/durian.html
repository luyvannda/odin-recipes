What is Durian?

Durian fruit is the edible fruit of several tree species belonging to the genus "Durio". It is distinctive for its large size, strong odour, and thorn-covered rind. It is commonly associated with Southeast Asian cuisine, especially in Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand, Cambodia, Philippines, Bangladesh and Vietnam. Durian is native to Borneo and Sumatra, but it is also cultivated in Cambodia. There are three varieties of durian that are traditional in Cambodia: the "Ov Khak", "Sadong Kit" and "Dounta". These are similar to some Indonesian varieties of durian. Durian is one of the most popular fruits in Cambodia...

Durian Crepe Recipe

Nothing beats the taste of durian pillow crepes, a delicious dessert made of paper-thin pancakes stuffed with whipped cream and durian chunks. You can easily whip up a batch of these heavenly treats at home and enjoy them chilled whenever you crave for something light and creamy.

Ingredients

  • 150g all-purpose flour
  • 330ml fresh milk
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter, melted
  • 2 eggs
  • 400g of durian pulp
  • 250ml of whipping cream
  • 1 table spoon caster sugar
  • Salt
    •

    Steps

    Without further ado, let us go to the steps of making a delicious durian crepe.

    1. Mix flour, milk, butter, eggs and a pinch of salt (optional) in a bowl until well mixed.
    2. Pour mixture over a sieve into another bowl to get a fine and smooth batter.
    3. Pour batter onto a lightly greased non-stick frying pan and swirl it around to get a very thin layer. Cook over a low fire until pancake solidifies.
    4. Repeat Step 3 until all the batter is finished.
    5. Add cold whipping cream and sugar into a bowl, then whip on high speed until it reaches stiff peaks.
    6. Peel and mash 400g of durian pulp, approximately 8-10 pieces depending on the size.
    7. Add mashed durian pulp into whipped cream mixture and mix gently.
    8. Take a tablespoon of durian whipped cream and place onto the center of a pancake, then fold all sides into the middle, forming it into a small, puffed pillow.
    9. Repeat Step 8 until all durian whipped cream is finished, then chill before serving.
